French Cooperation Department at RULE

Our Mission

The French Cooperation Department of RULE is the only academic department providing European accredited dual degrees in law, economics and management to students in Cambodia.

We offer internationally recognized programs in partnership with prestigious French universities, combining academic excellence with practical skills for the modern professional world.

Why Choose RULE?

  • Dual European-accredited degrees
  • International faculty and partnerships
  • Bilingual education (English/French)
  • Strong alumni network
  • Career-focused curriculum
