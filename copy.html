RULE and Aix-Marseille Université Conduct a Round-Table on ”Comparative Personal Data Protection Law in France and Cambodia: Cross Perspectives on the Legislative Contexts and Choices”

Posted on November 27, 2025November 27, 2025 by Paul Mornet
27
Nov
On Tuesday November 25th, the Dual LLM (Master of Law) in Digital Law RULE – Aix-Marseille University organized a Round-Table discussion on the theme ”Comparative Personal Data Protection Law in France and Cambodia: Cross Perspectives on the Legislative Contexts and Choices” at RULE. The discussions focused on the history of personal data protection law in France and Cambodia, the concepts consent and privacy and their legal materialisation, and the role of the judiciary and regulators in upholding data protection, through the various lenses of civil, public, private and criminal law.

A huge congratulation to the participants and panelists:

-Pr. Jean-Baptiste Perrier: Dean of the Faculté de droit et de science politique Aix-Marseille Université.

-Pr. Philippe Mouron: Full Professor and Director of the Master 2 Droit des Communications Electroniques (DCE), Aix-Marseille Université.

-Pr. Frédéric Laurie, Associate Professor, Aix-Marseille Université.

-Dr. Dr. Rothna NGORN NGORN, Adjunct Lecturer, Royal University of Law and Economics and Senior Associate, Bun & Associates.

-Mr. Pisal Chanty, Adjunct Lecturer, Royal University of Law and Economics, and Director, Digital & Innovation Policy Research, Cambodian Academy of Digital Technology.

-Asst. Pr. Paul Mornet, director of the program at RULE and moderator of the round-table.
