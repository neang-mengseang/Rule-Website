Directions

From Phnom Penh International Airport

Take National Highway 4 toward the city center. Turn right onto Monivong Boulevard and continue for approximately 8km. RULE is located on the left side near the intersection with Street 184.

Distance: ~15km | Time: 30-45 minutes by car

Public Transportation

Take bus routes 01, 02, or 03 that stop near the Central Market, then transfer to any bus heading south on Monivong Boulevard. Get off at the RULE stop.

Nearest landmarks: Central Market, Independence Monument

By Tuk-tuk or Taxi

Show the driver this address: Royal University of Law and Economics, Building D, Monivong Boulevard. Most drivers are familiar with the university.

GPS Coordinates: 11.5564° N, 104.9282° E
